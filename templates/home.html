ABOUT US & OUR BUSINESS

Charter Domain is a veteran-owned small business specializing in facility management, janitorial services, and grounds maintenance for government agencies. We hold a DoW Secret Clearance and operate both CONUS and OCONUS.

What separates us from standard facility contractors is where our standards come from. Our leadership was trained in U.S. Army Preventive Medicine and field sanitation — environments where cleanliness isn't a preference, it's a health and safety requirement. We apply that same clinical-grade standard to every contract we take on.

We've supported the U.S. Army, NATO, the Defense Health Agency, and the U.S. Department of War. We know government contracting, we know compliance, and we know what it takes to execute without fail.
